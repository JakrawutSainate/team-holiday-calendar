# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Team members, Team Leads, and Administrators at HolidayHQ who need to coordinate weekend/weekday shifts, plan leaves, view public holidays (integrated with the Bank of Thailand schedule), and monitor token ledger activities in a collaborative workspace.

## Product Purpose
HolidayHQ is an elegant, internal coordination tool that automates shift tracking, balances holiday token awards, locks public calendar dates automatically, and manages resource capacity dynamically to avoid team burnout.

## Brand Personality
- Minimal
- Precise
- Trustworthy

## Anti-references
- SaaS landing pages with gradient text and glowing badges.
- Cluttered grids of cards decorated with thin icons and tiny tracked eyebrows.
- Glassmorphic panels or side-stripe border decorations.

## Design Principles
- **Visual Comfort**: A calm, high-contrast off-white theme utilizing crisp slate and zinc scales to facilitate daily dashboard scans without visual fatigue.
- **Functional Integrity**: Elements look like what they do. Interactive cells and inputs have clear state transitions (hover, active, focus).
- **Rhythmic Simplicity**: Varied margins, clear font hierarchy, and structured layout alignment instead of crowded card frameworks.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Contrast ratios >= 4.5:1 for standard body text and form controls.
- Reduced motion consideration via CSS transitions that honor system settings.
